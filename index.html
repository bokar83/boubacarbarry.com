FAQ

What does “human-first AI” mean here?

Tools should reduce cognitive load and admin work. If they create more meetings or dashboards, we fix the system—not push harder on people.

How do engagements start?

Short audit → pilot with a small group → scale what works. Clear owners, simple metrics, no heroics.

Can you work with my internal team?

Yes. I pair with People Ops, Ops, or Product to build simple playbooks and leave them better than I found them.
